Version 1.0

Welcome to the Pinned Microsoft To Do lauching version!

What's new?

While you were using the beta version, some features have been developed that will make this addon easier to learn and simpler to use.

  • When you click on the Pinned Microsoft To Do icon at your toolbar, instead of opening a new tab on the background, it now opens on the foreground (the current tab is changed to the app).
  • If the app is already openned and you click on the Pinned Microsoft To Do icon again, instead of openning a new tab, it simply changes the current tab to the app's one.

It is also worth noting that the app url has been corrected because Microsoft has changed it recently. Now, instead of opening a welcome screen, you access "Your Day" list directly.

Usage

This addon aims to provide a quick and convinient way to open the Microsoft To Do app on your browser.

To do so, just click on the extension symbol on your toolbar (top right corner).
